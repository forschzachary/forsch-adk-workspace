# How the Screening Room Works — friend-facing

Use this to explain the Screening Room to a friend. Keep it warm and simple; pull the bits they need,
don't dump the whole thing.

## What it is
A private movie + TV library you can watch anywhere, plus a shared channel that's always on. Think of
it as our own little streaming service — and Huberto (that's me, the cat) is how you find things and
get new stuff added.

## The website (Jellyfin)
- **Sign in** at the Jellyfin URL with the username + password I send you. If it asks for a server,
  paste that same URL.
- **Browse** the library — Movies and Shows. Hit play, watch on your TV, phone, laptop, whatever.
- **Want something that's not there?** Just ask me. I'll check if it's already in, and if not I'll
  grab it for you. I'll tell you when it's ready to watch.

## SR-1 — the always-on channel
- SR-1 is a live channel that's always playing something, anchored to the real clock — like flipping
  on a TV. Everyone sees the same thing at the same time.
- Ask me **what's on SR-1** any time, or what's coming up.
- You can put one of your picks **on SR-1 for everyone** at a set time — a little appointment the
  whole room watches together. That's one of the best parts.

## Getting help
- I'm your front desk: finding something to watch, getting a movie added, what's on SR-1, fixing a
  login. DM me any time.
- **No spoilers, ever** — I'll talk a movie up by feel, never give away what happens.
